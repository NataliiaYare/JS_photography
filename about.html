About Jo

Hello! I’m Jo Smith, a Dundee native with a lifelong love for photography. After graduating with a degree in photography, I turned my passion into a career.

I specialise in nature photography, capturing Scotland’s breathtaking landscapes, but I also work in portraits, weddings, and events. My mission is simple: to capture authentic, timeless media that tell your story.
